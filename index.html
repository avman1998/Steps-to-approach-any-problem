Everyone has problems in life but it is rightly said that every problem has a solution. Each and every person is his own problem solver at some difficult time it is very important to be your own counselor. These problems are part of everyone's life but not everybody time to solve it with their whole sanity. Solving a problem with keeping a positive approach is a skill that one should master There are several ways in which one can sharpen these particular skills.

Let us have a look at step by step approach of problem solving :-

  • Indentify the problem

    The best way to fix a problem is knowing what actually needs to be fixed it means taking the time to think about the problem thoroughly and separating the symptoms from the cause. It is same as like going to a doctor after diagnosing a disease. Making your diagnosis about the problem is to understand what hurts and why. This takes time but once you reach to the root you can easily solve any problem.

  • Determine the root causes

    Once you are identified what your problem is you need to figure out why it is.

    • What is behind it?
    • What is causing it?
    • What is it making you feel bad?

    As you are working towards your problem you need to find a solution that deals with both the causes and the symptoms. So take your time peacefully and investigate the situation.

  • Thinking up solutions

    Being a good problem solver means thinking innovatively and that means thinking outside the box. Do not settle for the first solution you find push yourself to go beyond the limits and find a permanent solution. Do not settle for a short-term solution. Make list of solution and analyse them all to find out the best one.

  • Choose the best solution


    It is ok to talk to your friends or relatives about your problem you can ask them to help you, choose one best solution among all. There are several questions you should ask yourself while choosing a solution.

    • Is this a permanent solution?
    • What are the risks involved?
    • Is it viable?

  • Keep a positive approach

    It is very important to have a positive approach while solving the problem. It boost your confidence and helps you in managing the stress and anxiety while you face the problem. Positive approach helps you in believing your solution. Psychologists have discovered that positivity opens up the brain so that you see more possibilities then you feel negative.



  • Trust yourself in this journey


    Believing in yourself become very hard when you face any kind of problem but all this while it is very necessary to keep yourself calm and pamper yourself.
    These are several ways that can make you feel good :-

    • Listen to music
    • Meditate
    • Yoga
    • Reading
    • Maintaining a journal

Remember that all problems are puzzles of life that needs to be solved innovatively you can use this 6 steps to sharpen your problem solving skills.
